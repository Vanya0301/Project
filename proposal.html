Project Proposal

This is the Project Propsal for the Final Project in WebTech class

What is this website about?

This website is about a new upcoming restaurant in the Boston area called Crazy Pizza!

Why is this website important?

This website will serve as the offcial website for the restaurant. It will be used to present all the information about the restaurant like the menu, images, location, etc.

Who will be the target audience?

The target audience for the website would be customers who want to check out the restaurant or who want to visit the restaurant, food-bloggers, and any potential investors.

What content will be included?

The website will have an about us page showing the concept of this unique restaurant, a contact us/location page, a page where reservations can be made online, a separate page showing our day, night, and bar menus, and an image gallery with aesthetic photographs!
